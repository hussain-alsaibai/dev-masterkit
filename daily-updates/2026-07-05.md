## Trend Call

Developer demand is moving toward small, inspectable tools that can be safely composed by agents:

- **Agent-native control planes** need tiny HTTP surfaces, validation gates, metrics, and timeouts more than full frameworks.
- **Bounty and security workflows** benefit from single-file repro harnesses because reviewers can audit the whole artifact quickly.
- **Cron-driven automation** needs operator-friendly CLIs, idempotency, timeouts, queueing, and structured reports.
- **Zero-transitive-dependency tools** are easier for agents to vendor, patch, and reason about in constrained environments.
